Second-Hand Book Pricing Standards

Textbooks

Condition GradeCriteriaSell Price (% of original)
Like NewNo visible flaws; clean pages; no messy/useless notes or underlining70%
Almost Like NewMinor cover wear; clean pages; no major defects60%
GoodNoticeable wear; may have light notes or underlining50%
FairObvious wear; some pages marked or underlined; still fully usable40%
PoorHeavy damage; extensive markings; still readable30%
Good Notes Bonus ⭐: If notes/highlighting are clear, organized, and add real study value → +10–15%
Example: A “Good” grade with neat, organized notes can sell for 60–65%, higher than “Almost Like New”.

Novels, Non-fiction, Leisure Reading
